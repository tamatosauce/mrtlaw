Zikhona Ndlebe holds an LLB degree from the University of the Western Cape (UWC) and is currently completing her LLM specialising in Labour Law at the University of Cape Town (UCT). She is an admitted attorney of the High Court of South Africa with a High Court Right of Appearance. She specialises in Labour and Employment Law related matters. These include matters relating to unfair dismissals, unfair labour practices, high-level disciplinary issues and disputes, organisational rights, restraint of trade and industrial action disputes.

Zikhona also has extensive experience in drafting employment agreements, policies and procedures and in providing training to corporate entities on various employment related topics, including acting as a Disciplinary Chairperson in various disciplinary hearings.

Zikhona regularly represents clients at the Commission for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA), various Bargaining Councils and at the Labour Court.
